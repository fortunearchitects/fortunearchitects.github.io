Investor Education and Awareness

Distributors explain the concept of mutual funds, their types, and associated risks and benefits. They help investors understand their own financial goals, risk tolerance, and investment horizon.

Investment Selection and Recommendation

Based on investor profiles, distributors recommend suitable mutual fund schemes from a variety of Asset Management Companies (AMCs). They compare different fund options, highlighting their features, performance history, and investment styles.

Transaction Facilitation

Distributors assist investors in purchasing, redeeming, or switching between different mutual fund units. They handle the paperwork and ensure transactions are completed smoothly.

Ongoing Support and Guidance

Distributors provide ongoing support by monitoring investment performance and keeping investors informed about market trends. They answer investor queries and address any concerns related to their investments.

Adherence to Regulations

Mutual fund distributors are obligated to follow regulations set by the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) and the Association of Mutual Funds in India (AMFI). This ensures ethical practices and fair representation of various investment options.
