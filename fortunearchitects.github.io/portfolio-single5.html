Bucket Elevator

This workhorse of the material handling world excels at vertically transporting bulk materials. Imagine a series of buckets attached to a continuous chain or belt, scooping up and lifting materials to their designated heights. Fortune Architects' bucket elevators are likely customizable to handle a variety of materials, capacities, and vertical distances, making them ideal for applications in power plants, grain storage facilities, and chemical processing plants.

Screw Conveyor

For efficient horizontal or inclined transportation of bulk materials, Fortune Architects offers screw conveyors. A rotating helical screw flight within an enclosed trough continuously pushes the material forward. Screw conveyors are known for their gentle handling and ability to navigate tight spaces, making them suitable for applications in food processing, plastic manufacturing, and even animal feed production.

Overhead Chain Conveyor

This versatile system utilizes a chain suspended from an overhead track to transport materials. Products can be hung directly from the chain or placed on carriers attached to the chain. Fortune Architects' overhead chain conveyors likely offer customization options for layout, speed, and weight capacity, making them perfect for assembly lines, paint shops, and warehouses with overhead space.

Roller Conveyor

A simple yet effective solution for conveying products along a horizontal or slightly inclined path, Fortune Architects' roller conveyors consist of a series of free-rolling cylinders mounted on a sturdy frame. These conveyors are ideal for transporting boxes, totes, and pallets efficiently and smoothly, making them popular choices in packaging lines, distribution centers, and manufacturing facilities.

Portable Belt Conveyor

Fortune Architects caters to temporary or flexible material handling needs with their portable belt conveyors. These self-contained units are likely powered by electric motors and feature a conveyor belt that efficiently transports materials over short distances. Portable belt conveyors are perfect for loading and unloading trucks, moving materials around construction sites, or providing temporary conveying solutions in production lines.

Bagasse Bale Breaker

If your industry deals with bagasse, the fibrous residue left after sugarcane crushing, Fortune Architects has you covered. Their bagasse bale breakers are designed to break down compacted bagasse bales into a manageable size, facilitating further processing or transportation. This equipment is crucial for sugar mills and biofuel plants that utilize bagasse as a fuel source.

Coal Handling System

Fortune Architects understands the importance of safe and efficient coal handling. Their coal handling systems likely encompass a comprehensive suite of equipment, including conveyors, crushers, and dust suppression systems, to ensure the smooth and controlled movement of coal from storage to its point of use in power plants or industrial facilities.
